Internet and Hardware

Our venue is souped up with 1 Gb/s internet. These speeds are going to be available through Wi-Fi, along with ethernet switches and wired connections. If needed for your hack, we will also have some equipment available for loan including:

  • Myos
  • Sparkcores
  • Tessels
  • Pebbles

Judging Criteria

Hacks will be judged based on the criteria below:

  • Creativity
  • Technical Difficulty
  • Usefulness
  • Design

Hacks can be built on any platform/language/API, although our sponsors will be giving out specific prizes for the best hacks using their platforms/APIs. These special prizes will be announced during the opening ceremonies. There will also be seperate prizes for top sustainable and hardware hack, along with the overall prize.

Basic Rules

  • Follow our Code of Conduct
  • Be courteous to your fellow hackers, mentors, judges, reps, and the nwHacks crew
  • Follow directions given to you by our staff
  • Hacks MUST be built from scratch. (You can use libraries, engines and APIs but you cannot implement core functionality of your hack until announcement is given)
  • Failure to follow these rules and those given by staff will result in disqualification

What is nwHacks?

nwHacks is one of the biggest student-run hackathons in the Pacific Northwest. It creates a fun and awesome venue for coding while bringing together the wide student community of expert developers and developers-to-be.

That’s great and all but what do you actually do in a “hackathon” anyway?

No, it’s not what you think - you’re not going to try and access your friend's Facebook account. Instead, you’ll be doing something better and far more interesting! In a span of 36 hours, you will be collaborating with a team of 2-5 people in developing an app!

Developing an app??? But I don’t even know a thing or two about coding!

Doesn’t matter! We've got your back! We’ll make sure to put you in a team where an experienced developer could guide you. All you really need is your laptop, overnight stuff, and your thinking cap! Nothing else.

Who participates in this?

Apart from YOU? Why, all the best students who have a budding interest in tech, of course! Students anywhere from Arts to Engineering, Commerce to Science, Forestry to Kinesiology are all welcome to nwHacks, whether it’s their first time coding or feel like they’ve done it one too many times!
If you are a college dropout or a highschooler, please email us at apps@nwhacks.com to apply.
